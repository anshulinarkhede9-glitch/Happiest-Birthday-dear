## ✨ Features

✅ **Pinkish Lip Kisses Background** - Animated, floating lip emojis (💋 👄) throughout the background  
✅ **5 Interactive Slides** - Welcome message, 3 photo slides, and a final celebratory message  
✅ **Cute Birthday Messages** - Personalized messages with emojis on each slide  
✅ **Photo Integration** - Easily embed photos from Google Photos or any image URL  
✅ **Auto-Play Functionality** - Slides automatically change every 8 seconds  
✅ **Multiple Navigation Options**:
   - Next/Back buttons
   - Dot indicators (click to jump to any slide)
   - Keyboard arrows (← →)  
✅ **Floating Hearts Animation** - Hearts float up when changing slides  
✅ **Smooth Transitions** - Elegant fade-in/fade-out effects  
✅ **Fully Responsive** - Works perfectly on mobile, tablet, and desktop  
✅ **No Database Required** - Pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript  
✅ **Easy to Customize** - Simple color schemes, messages, and layouts  

---


Simply open `index.html` in your web browser to see the website in action!

**Try it:** 
- Click the "Next" and "Back" buttons to navigate
- Click the dots at the bottom to jump to a specific slide
- Use arrow keys on keyboard for navigation
- Watch the slides auto-play and hearts float across the screen
